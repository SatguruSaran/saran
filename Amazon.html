Amazon Prime Kitchen

Fall 2017

  • Single Edit
  • Column Edit

Design Challenge: Design a new Amazon customer delivery experience that grows the subscriber base, and from that, derive the new operational systems required to support the business.

Our Idea

Press Release: Introducing a novel automated delivery system to deliver healthy and fresh food in a hassle free and optimized way.

Team: 
Saran & Echo Lu- Product Managers
Rahul Jain- Design Manager
Pratik- Operations Manager

Role Description: As a Product Manager,

  • I lead the team and understand overall landscape
  • Design SWOT/Business Assessment
  • Cost structure and Revenue stream forecast
  • Select overall strategy

    Target audience

    Major users of the product would be young working professionals or college students living in big cities who only cook for themselves and are willing to order food and groceries online.

  • 18-35 year old
  • Working professionals & students
  • Familiar with online ordering
  • Live alone or shared apartments
  • Often eat outside and have no time to cook

    Solution Features

    Automatic Guided Vehicle
  • Self-Driving
  • Location Tracking
  • Multiple Orders
  • Route Optimization
  • Real-time Updates

    • Smart Mailbox
  • GPS Locator
  • Temperature control
  • Notifications
  • Smart Lock
  • Weather Sealed

    • Platform Features

  • Calorie calculator in the cart
  • Know the nutrition facts about your meal
  • Build personal profile in your Amazon Prime Kitchen account

    • Mobile Application Design

    Value for the Customer

  • No need to stay home for delivery
  • Saves time in ordering
  • Free mailbox with yearly subscription
  • Food remains fresh & healthy
  • Users can get groceries or prepared meals according to their preference
  • Users can track calorie count
  • Real time updates & notifications
  • Shorter & accurate delivery time

    • Value for the Business

  • Less chances of returns, food going bad, stolen and missing food
  • Ensures safety and healthy food
  • Cost effective in longer run
  • Less chances of manual errors & accidents
  • Automated process for order & delivery
  • Can be expanded to all products on Amazon
